RoboEdify offers both online and offline classes in coding and circuit building, providing flexible learning options for students of all ages. The programs cover coding fundamentals (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, etc.) and circuit-building skills, including hands-on experience with hardware like Arduino systems. Online classes feature interactive sessions using tools like Tinkercad for simulations, while offline classes offer direct access to equipment and real-world hardware projects, fostering practical learning. With a project-based approach, students can build integrated applications like smart home systems or programmable robots, supported by experienced mentors who tailor the curriculum to individual skill levels and interests.

Classes are designed to be flexible and accommodate students' schedules, especially on weekends when most students have more free time. The programs focus on interactive, hands-on learning in areas like coding, circuit building, and understanding electronic components, catering to various age groups and skill levels.

Weekend-Focused Schedule: Most classes are conducted on weekends to ensure students can participate without interfering with their regular school or weekday activities.

Custom Timing Options: Classes can be tailored to suit students' available time slots, ensuring they can join sessions comfortably and make the most of their learning experience.

RoboEdify offers one-hour weekend classes designed to fit around regular academics, providing focused sessions on coding, circuit building, and more, perfect for kids and hobbyists
