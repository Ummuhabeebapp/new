RoboEdify offers both online and offline classes in coding and circuit building, providing flexible learning options for students of all ages. The programs cover coding fundamentals (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, etc.) and circuit-building skills, including hands-on experience with hardware like Arduino systems. Online classes feature interactive sessions using tools like Tinkercad for simulations, while offline classes offer direct access to equipment and real-world hardware projects, fostering practical learning. With a project-based approach, students can build integrated applications like smart home systems or programmable robots, supported by experienced mentors who tailor the curriculum to individual skill levels and interests.
